Week 1: [03/10/2016-07/10/2016]

Intro to Activity-Led Learning (ALL). Introduction to the group. We familiarised ourselves with the equipment provided and verified that it matched the inventory on the Equipment Loan Sheet. After introductions we began tinkering with the componenets. Skills-set analysis happened fairly organically when a sub-group accidentally spaced the wheels to far apart for the tank tread. We agreed to model out robot after TRACK3R for it's simplicity. Eventually we contructed a bed to mount the Programmable Brick onto. Two large motors were attatched to the wheel axles eitherside. After that we familiarised ourselves with the Lego visual programming language and were able to get the brick to run some very basic programmes.
